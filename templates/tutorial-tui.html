üí° Tips & Best Practices

üìè Terminal Size

Use a terminal at least 80x24 characters. Larger terminals provide better visibility and navigation.

üîÑ Session Management

The TUI validates your session periodically. If your session expires, simply run kg-cli login again.

‚å®Ô∏è Vim Users

If you're familiar with Vim, you'll feel right at home! Use j/k for navigation and familiar keybindings.

üìä Daily Dashboard

Start your day by pressing d to see your stats and trending notes!

üîç Quick Search

From any view, press / to instantly search your notes.

üï∏Ô∏è Explore Connections

Press g to see how your notes are connected. Discover relationships you forgot about!

üö® Troubleshooting

"Session expired" error
Run kg-cli login to refresh your authentication token.
Terminal too small
Resize your terminal to at least 80x24 characters. The TUI requires minimum space to render properly.
TUI won't start
Make sure you're authenticated with kg-cli status and the API is accessible.
Display looks garbled
Some terminals don't support advanced TUI features. Try a modern terminal like iTerm2, GNOME Terminal, or Windows Terminal.
